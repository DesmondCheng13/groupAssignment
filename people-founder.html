Tay Keng Lee
Founder
Mr. Tay Keng Lee is a distinguished individual known for his significant contributions in the field of education and cultural enrichment.

As the founder of 24 Seasons Drum at SMK Seri Kembangan, he played a pivotal role in introducing the art of drumming to the institution, fostering creativity, and promoting cultural understanding among students. Serving as the Principal of SMK Seri Kembangan, Mr. Tay's visionary leadership left an indelible mark on the school community, nurturing academic excellence and character development.

Having dedicated years of his life to the betterment of education and cultural exchange, Mr. Tay Keng Lee retired in 2019, leaving behind a legacy of innovation and inspiration.

His passion for both education and drumming has not only impacted the lives of countless students but has also enriched the cultural fabric of the institution. Through his tenure and leadership, Mr. Tay has left an enduring legacy that continues to resonate and shape the educational landscape.
