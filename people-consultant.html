Founder Consultant Coach
Lim Ewe Guan
Consultant
Mr. Lim Ewe Guan is a distinguished educator known for his instrumental role at SMK Seri Kembangan as a Consultant Teacher for the 24 Season Drum. With a wealth of experience, he also served as the Head of the English Department, contributing significantly to the school's academic excellence and cultural enrichment.

Under his guidance, the 24 Season Drum team flourished, attaining remarkable achievements and recognition both within and beyond the school's walls. Mr. Lim's visionary leadership extended beyond the classroom, as he actively led the team to mesmerizing performances at various external venues, fostering a wider appreciation for the art of drumming.

Mr. Lim's legacy is deeply intertwined with the school's growth and vibrancy, as he dedicated himself to nurturing both students' academic pursuits and their artistic talents. His tenure culminated in a well-deserved retirement in 2020, marking the end of an era characterized by his unwavering dedication, innovative approach, and profound impact on the school community.
